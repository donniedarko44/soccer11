About Us

A DIFFERENT
KIND OF AGENT

Football management is about relationships, timing, and an uncompromising eye for talent. Adrian Aliaj founded Soccer Eleven with one conviction: every player deserves an agent who fights for them the way a coach fights for a win.

With over 15 years of experience across European football, Adrian has built a network that reaches from the Adriatic coast to the Bundesliga, the Premier League, and beyond. Soccer Eleven's approach is personal, direct, and relentlessly professional.

We handle transfers, contract negotiations, player development planning, and long-term career strategy — so our players can focus entirely on the pitch.
